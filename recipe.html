Shrimp Spaghetti

Delicious spaghetti pasta with succulent shrimp in a savory tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 oz spaghetti pasta
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Directions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes. Sauté until garlic is fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp to the skillet and cook until they turn pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce and simmer for 5-7 minutes, allowing flavors to meld together.
  5. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat evenly with the sauce.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh parsley.

Chicken Biryani

A flavorful and aromatic Indian rice dish with marinated chicken and spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Directions

  1. Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Let it sit for 1 hour.
  3. Heat oil in a large pot. Add the bay leaves and cinnamon stick. Sauté for a few seconds.
  4. Add the onions and cook until golden brown. Add the marinated chicken and cook until the chicken is no longer pink.
  5. Add the rice and mix well with the chicken and spices. Add enough water to cook the rice. Bring it to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  7. Fluff the rice gently before serving.

Lasagna

Classic Italian lasagna with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and creamy cheese filling.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium-high heat until browned. Add onion and garlic, and cook until onions are translucent.
  3. Add marinara sauce to the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, and chopped parsley. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Arrange 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle with 1 cup of mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining meat sauce and sprinkle with remaining mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, until bubbly and golden.
  9. Let lasagna stand for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Patty Burger

Juicy beef patty with fresh vegetables and savory sauces, sandwiched in a toasted bun.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns, split and toasted
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 slices tomato
  • 4 slices red onion
  • 4 slices pickles
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Season ground beef with salt and pepper. Form into 4 patties.
  3. Grill patties for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked to desired doneness.
  4. Toast hamburger buns on the grill or skillet until lightly browned.
  5. Spread mayonnaise on the bottom half of each bun. Top with lettuce, tomato slices, onion slices, and pickles.
  6. Place cooked patties on top of the vegetables. Add a slice of cheese on each patty.
  7. Spread ketchup and mustard on the inside of the top bun. Place on top of the cheese.
  8. Serve immediately.

Salad

Fresh and healthy salad with mixed greens, vegetables, and vinaigrette dressing.

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens
  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Red onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Carrots
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. In a large bowl, toss together mixed greens, cucumbers, tomatoes, red onions, bell peppers, and carrots.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve chilled.

Paneer Butter Masala

Creamy and flavorful paneer cubes cooked in a rich buttery tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Butter
  • Tomatoes
  • Cashews
  • Heavy cream
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, garam masala)
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Prepare the paneer by frying or soaking in hot water.
  2. Blend tomatoes, cashews, and spices into a smooth paste.
  3. Heat butter in a pan, add ginger-garlic paste, and sauté.
  4. Pour the tomato-cashew paste and cook until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add paneer cubes and cream. Simmer until flavors blend.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan or rice.

Veg Patty Burger

Delicious veggie patty with fresh vegetables and sauces, sandwiched in a toasted bun.

Ingredients

  • Veggie patty (made with beans, peas, and carrots)
  • Hamburger buns, split and toasted
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced red onions
  • Pickles
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Heat veggie patty on a grill or skillet until heated through.
  2. Toast hamburger buns until lightly browned.
  3. Spread mayonnaise on the bottom half of each bun.
  4. Layer with lettuce, tomato slices, red onion slices, pickles, and a veggie patty.
  5. Add a slice of cheddar cheese on top of the patty.
  6. Spread ketchup and mustard on the inside of the top bun.
  7. Place on top of the cheese and assemble the burger.
  8. Serve immediately.

French Fries

Crispy and golden French fries, perfect as a side or snack.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt
  • Optional additional ingredients:
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Chili powder
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh herbs like parsley or rosemary

Directions

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into strips.
  2. Soak in cold water for 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  3. Drain and pat dry with paper towels.
  4. Heat oil in a deep fryer or skillet.
  5. Fry potatoes in batches until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels.
  7. Season with salt while hot and serve immediately.
